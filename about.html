WHO I AM

Hello, I'm Kevin Lamprecht.​​​​​​​​​​

I initially became interested in games at a young age; while other kids realized that they only enjoyed games for fun and not for work, I started to really examine games. I specifically started to examine how level design works, and how the placement of objects and parts of the environment can lead a player to discover something without actually telling them directly. This was what started my interest in game development, but I also started to begin to examine the animations of characters, and realized I would like to work on creating realistic looking animations for character’s actions such as running, walking, swimming etc.
